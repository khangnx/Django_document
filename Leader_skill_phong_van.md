# 📋 Software Engineer Leader / Team Lead Interview Q&A (Senior/Lead Level)

---

## 1. Leadership mindset

### ❓ Question  
What is a good leader?

### 🇻🇳 Trả lời (Basic)  
Một leader tốt không chỉ giỏi kỹ thuật mà còn biết hỗ trợ team phát triển.

### 🚀 🇻🇳 Nâng cao (Senior/Lead)  
Một leader tốt tạo ra impact thông qua team. Tôi tập trung vào việc định hướng kỹ thuật, xây dựng culture (ownership, accountability) và giúp team scale. Thành công của leader là khi team hoạt động hiệu quả ngay cả khi không cần phụ thuộc vào leader.

### 🇬🇧 Answer (Advanced)  
A strong leader creates impact through the team. I focus on technical direction, building a culture of ownership and accountability, and enabling the team to scale. A great leader succeeds when the team performs well even without direct dependency on them.

---

## 2. Team management

### ❓ Question  
How do you handle an underperforming developer?

### 🇻🇳 Trả lời (Basic)  
Trao đổi 1-1 và đưa ra plan cải thiện.

### 🚀 🇻🇳 Nâng cao  
Tôi xác định root cause (skill gap, unclear expectation, burnout). Sau đó xây dựng measurable improvement plan (KPIs rõ ràng). Tôi theo dõi progress theo tuần và provide feedback liên tục. Nếu không cải thiện, tôi đưa ra quyết định dựa trên team impact.

### 🇬🇧 Answer (Advanced)  
I identify the root cause (skill gap, unclear expectations, or burnout), then create a measurable improvement plan with clear KPIs. I track progress weekly and provide continuous feedback. If there is no improvement, I make decisions based on team impact.

---

## 3. Conflict resolution

### ❓ Question  
How do you resolve conflict between team members?

### 🚀 🇻🇳 Nâng cao  
Tôi tạo safe space để cả hai bên chia sẻ. Sau đó align discussion về goal chung của team. Nếu cần, tôi chuyển debate sang data-driven (benchmark, performance). Mục tiêu không phải là “ai đúng”, mà là solution tốt nhất cho hệ thống.

### 🇬🇧 Answer (Advanced)  
I create a safe space for both sides to share their perspectives, then align the discussion with the team's shared goals. If needed, I shift the debate to data-driven analysis. The goal is not who is right, but what solution is best.

---

## 4. Technical leadership

### ❓ Question  
How do you ensure code quality?

### 🚀 🇻🇳 Nâng cao  
Tôi xây dựng quality system thay vì phụ thuộc cá nhân:
- Coding standards + linting
- Mandatory code review
- CI/CD pipeline (test + check)
- Define “Definition of Done”

Ngoài ra, tôi promote engineering culture như clean code, refactoring và ownership.

### 🇬🇧 Answer (Advanced)  
I build a quality system rather than relying on individuals:
- Coding standards and linting  
- Mandatory code reviews  
- CI/CD pipelines with automated tests  
- Clear Definition of Done  

I also promote engineering culture such as clean code, refactoring, and ownership.

---

## 5. Decision making

### ❓ Question  
What if your team disagrees on a technical solution?

### 🚀 🇻🇳 Nâng cao  
Tôi dùng framework:
1. Collect options  
2. Evaluate (performance, scalability, cost)  
3. Time-box decision  

Nếu cần, tôi là người quyết định cuối cùng. Quan trọng là commit sau decision và tránh kéo dài debate.

### 🇬🇧 Answer (Advanced)  
I use a structured approach:
1. Collect options  
2. Evaluate trade-offs (performance, scalability, cost)  
3. Time-box the decision  

If needed, I make the final call. The key is to commit after the decision and avoid prolonged debates.

## 6. Deadline pressure

### ❓ Question  
What do you do when a project is delayed?

### 🚀 🇻🇳 Nâng cao  
Tôi không chỉ fix mà còn prevent:
- Re-scope (MVP mindset)  
- Re-estimate với data thực tế  
- Improve planning process  

Quan trọng nhất là communicate sớm với stakeholder để tránh mất trust.

### 🇬🇧 Answer (Advanced)  
I focus not only on fixing but also preventing:
- Re-scoping with MVP mindset  
- Re-estimating based on actual data  
- Improving planning processes  

Most importantly, I communicate early with stakeholders to maintain trust.

## 7. Mentoring

### ❓ Question  
Do you have mentoring experience?

### 🚀 🇻🇳 Nâng cao  
Tôi không chỉ mentor kỹ thuật mà còn mindset:
- Ownership  
- Problem-solving  
- Communication  

Tôi giúp junior trở thành mid-level, và mid-level trở thành senior.

### 🇬🇧 Answer (Advanced)  
I mentor not only technical skills but also mindset:
- Ownership  
- Problem-solving  
- Communication  

I help juniors grow into mid-level engineers and mid-level engineers become seniors.

## 8. Ownership

### ❓ Question  
What do you do if a project fails?

### 🚀 🇻🇳 Nâng cao  
Tôi chịu trách nhiệm end-to-end:
- Conduct post-mortem (không blame)  
- Identify system/process issues  
- Apply improvements  

Failure là cơ hội để nâng cấp system.

### 🇬🇧 Answer (Advanced)  
I take end-to-end responsibility:
- Conduct blameless post-mortems  
- Identify system/process issues  
- Apply improvements  

Failure is an opportunity to improve the system.

---

## 9. Communication

### ❓ Question  
How do you explain technical concepts to non-technical people?

### 🚀 🇻🇳 Nâng cao  
Tôi translate từ “technical detail” → “business impact”.  
Ví dụ: thay vì nói latency, tôi nói “user phải chờ bao lâu và ảnh hưởng conversion thế nào”.

### 🇬🇧 Answer (Advanced)  
I translate technical details into business impact.  
For example, instead of discussing latency, I explain how it affects user experience and conversion.

---

## 10. Motivation

### ❓ Question  
How do you motivate your team?

### 🚀 🇻🇳 Nâng cao  
Tôi không motivate bằng lời nói mà bằng system:
- Clear goal  
- Recognition  
- Growth path  

Motivation bền vững đến từ việc thấy mình đang phát triển.

### 🇬🇧 Answer (Advanced)  
I don’t rely on words alone but build a system:
- Clear goals  
- Recognition  
- Growth path  

Sustainable motivation comes from seeing personal growth.
